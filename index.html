Introduction to E-Waste

The increased use of electronic devices has caused a rise in the generation of e-waste, which is detrimental to t he environment and human health. This study aimed to assess consumer awareness, perception, and disposal methods of e-waste management and its determinants in a semi-urban area of northern Tamil Nadu.

In this project, we'll explore how consumer behavior contributes to the e-waste problem, focusing on three key aspects:

  • Planned obsolescence in electronics
  • Frequent product upgrades and their impact
  • Disposal habits of tech consumers
